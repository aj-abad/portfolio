For my capstone project, my team had to deliver a product that will help barber shops partially regain their lost income due to the COVID-19 pandemic. We had to develop a minumum viable product and defend it within three months.
$
My team came up with Cura, a Vue- and .NET-powered PWA that helps personal care shops do home service. Shop owners can use the application to manage their services, while customers can browse through available shops and book services from them.
